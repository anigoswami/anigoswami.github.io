1.

Language sure is MAGIC

For as long as I can remember, I have never been able to give a clear, concise answer as to what I want to do in life. The question always seemed too vague to me. How would I know what the 'me' of tomorrow might like? I have no control over the future! And what if I decide something now, but change it later on ? Like most kids in school, I too was asked to write essays on 'My Aim In Life '. I wrote about a different profession every single time. I remember in fourth grade, I wrote about how I wanted to be a singer/song-writer! In sixth grade, I wanted to be an astronaut. By the time I was in eighth grade, thanks to Masterchef, I had decided that food was where I would find my calling, and that I should become a chef.
